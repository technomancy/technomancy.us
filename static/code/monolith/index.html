Monolith

A game.

Rules

The object is to capture all of your opponent's pieces. You may move any of your pieces one square in any direction. You may move onto another piece provided:

  • the target piece is your color or
  • the target piece has a "1" on it or
  • the target piece's number is less than the capturing piece's number minus one
